The Process
How We Work

The 7-Step Process — All Divisions

One battle-tested sequence across real estate, aircraft, and yacht. Zero guesswork from handshake to closing.

01
Broker Agreement
Signed upfront before any work begins. Commission locked in writing. Protects both parties from day one.
02
Asset Dossier
Real estate: deed, title, property condition. Aircraft: N-number, logbooks, total time. Yacht: USCG docs, HIN, survey history.
03
Title Search
County recorder (real estate), FAA Registry (aircraft), USCG Documentation Center (yacht). Zero liens confirmed before listing.
04
Market Launch
Deployed across all primary platforms for each division plus our private buyer network. Professional listing included.
05
Buyer Qualification
Every inquiry screened. Proof of funds required before a showing is confirmed. Only verified serious buyers reach the seller.
06
Inspection / Survey
Real estate: property inspection. Aircraft: A&P mechanic PPI. Yacht: NAMS/SAMS marine survey and sea trial. All findings negotiated.
07
Escrow & Close
Funds secured in escrow. Title transfers cleanly. Asset delivered. Commission collected. Deal closed and documented.
